1. Description of Service

SocialMockup is a design utility application designed to run inside the Canva Editor. It allows users to generate highly realistic social media post mockups (X/Twitter, Instagram Post & Story, LinkedIn, Facebook, Threads, and YouTube comment templates) with custom usernames, avatars, text contents, media attachments, and metrics. All mockup rendering calculations are completed locally within your browser tab session.
