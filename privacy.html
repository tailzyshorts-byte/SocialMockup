Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

At SocialMockup, we take your privacy extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the SocialMockup application (the "App") inside the Canva platform.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SocialMockup is designed as a browser-first, privacy-respecting tool. We do not run external database servers to store your files, custom texts, or profile credentials. Consequently:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
  • No Media & Text Storage: The custom usernames, handles, and avatars you input or upload for post mockups are processed locally on your device via HTML Canvas APIs. We do not transfer or save your source media or rendered screenshots on any external servers owned by us.

2. Asset Upload and Canva Integration

When you click the "Add Mockup to Design" button, the App utilizes official Canva SDK APIs to upload the generated screenshots directly to your personal Canva media library. This transaction is governed strictly by Canva's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. We have no access to your Canva account or design content.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

The App may use standard browser technologies (such as Session Storage or Local Storage) to maintain temporary application state (e.g. your active form inputs or platform settings) while the application is active in your browser tab. These tokens are local to your browser session and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

4. Security

Because all mockup rendering and graphic calculations occur locally in your web browser, your input data never leaves your environment until you choose to upload the output to Canva. We encourage users to maintain secure browser environments.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please visit our Support Page to get in touch with our team.
